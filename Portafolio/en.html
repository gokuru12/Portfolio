Chrono Defiance: Last Stand places you in the role of an inventor whose machines have turned against humanity. Survive for as long as possible while hostile robots slowly drain your remaining time. Focus: Level design and experimental mechanics

Holly Meow is a 2D platformer and one of my earliest experiences in game development. The player controls a man with the ability to transform into a feline. Trapped inside a church, the character must use both human skills and feline abilities to escape.

This was my first university project and received an award for originality despite being developed by the smallest team. My main responsibilities included designing all core mechanics, defining character abilities for both forms, and creating levels that supported a satisfying player experience.
