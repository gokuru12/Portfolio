Narrative Design Samples

This page showcases examples of narrative design elements I have created for various projects, including NPC barks, tooltips, tutorial prompts, and branching dialogue. These samples demonstrate my ability to craft engaging and informative narrative content that enhances player immersion and storytelling within games.
